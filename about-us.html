About Us 
关于我们

Gondesan Great Merciful Water Association is a Buddhist Religious Organization that follows the spirit of dharma school of Avalokitesvara. We spread the gospel of Buddha dharma to purify the human soul, to assist people in need, to eradicate evil, and to pray for peace on Earth to achieve social harmony.
功德山寺大悲水協會是一個佛教宗教組織，秉承觀世音菩薩學校的精神。我們傳播佛法的福音，以淨化人的靈魂，幫助有需要的人，消除邪惡，並為地球上的和平祈禱以實現社會和諧


Master Kuan Ru

Master Kuan Ru is the founder of Gondesan. Her legal name is Shi Kuanru, and she is a native of Chiayi Taiwan. She holds a PhD in Buddhism. She travels around the world to spread her teachings, and she holds recurring events in major cities such as Taiwan, New York, and Toronto.
寬如法師是功德山的創始人, 她是台灣嘉義人。她擁有佛教博士學位。功德山中華國際大悲咒水功德會-寬如法師功德山以利益一切廣大有情眾生為弘法志業，秉承南無本師釋迦牟尼佛之教法延續 .
